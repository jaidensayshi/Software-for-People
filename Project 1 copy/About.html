Welcome to Hush House!

Books are the memory that does not die.

Hush House is a library with a long and storied history, one of only nine libraries where mortals are permitted to study forbidden texts. (Of course, mortals have found ways to study such texts outside of these libraries, but Hush House's importance remains).

This House is the centerpiece of Weather Factory's game Book of Hours, which I absolutely adore. Collected here is a selection of books from that game; those which I found particularly interesting or which I used frequently while playing. 1 Return to library
